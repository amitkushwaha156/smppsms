DLT Registration

Dear Valuable Client,

As per the TRAI guidelines issued on 19th July, 2018 DLT has been implemented by all the Operators (BSNL, Videocon, Vodafone, Jio, Airtel) from 1st of June 2020. So now it is mandatory for all to register their Entity on DLT Platform and complete their Entity and Header (Sender ID) registration for pushing traffic on DLT.

Smart Technology - SMPPSMShub is already registered as a Telemarketer on the DLT Platform of the Operators. All Re-sellers, SMPP Providers and Users have to register themselves as Enterprise using our DLT Telemarketer ID.

From 1st June Messages from DLT Registered Entity will be allowed and Messages from non registered Entity will be impacted.

If you are registered on any of the Operator (Videocon, BSNL, Vodafone, Jio, Airtel) we request you to register you Entity Headers (Sender IDs) on the DLT Platform.



Below are the Operator’s DLT Portals
S.NO. DLT Portal Link
1 BSNL https://www.ucc-bsnl.co.in/
2 Videocon https://smartping.live/entity/signup/Indian/
3 Vodafone https://www.vilpower.in/
4 Airtel https://dltconnect.airtel.in/
5 MTNL https://www.ucc-mtnl.in/
6 Jio https://trueconnect.jio.com


Each User, Reseller and SMPP User can register as Enterprise under our Telemarketer ID as below. Please select Smart Technology Telemarketer ID which is available in the DLT Portal.

S.NO. DLT Portal Telemarketer Name Telemarketer ID
1 BSNL Smart Technology 1402673970000010012
2 Videocon Smart Technology 1402673970000010012
3 Vodafone Smart Technology 1402673970000010012
4 Jio Smart Technology 1402673970000010012

Once you have registered on any one of the DLT Portal, you have to register your Headers (Sender IDs) in the Operator’s DLT Portal. Your SMS will get delivered only with the Headers (Sender IDs) which are approved in the DLT Portal of the Operator.

There are no fees charged at the DLT Portal for the registration of Entity.
