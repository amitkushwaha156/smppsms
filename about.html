Bulk SMS

Bulk SMS is a service that allows you to send a large number of SMS messages to a group of recipients

RCS

Rich Communication Services is a protocol that enhances traditional SMS messaging with rich media capabilities.

Voice

Voice services allow users to make phone calls over the internet, enabling communication through voice calls.

IVR

Interactive Voice Response (IVR) is a technology that allows users to interact with a computer system through voice commands or keypad inputs.

Click to Call

Click to Call is a feature that allows users to initiate a phone call by clicking on a link or button, typically on a website or application.

Missed Call is a service that allows users to receive notifications or alerts when they miss a phone call, often used for verification or confirmation purposes.
